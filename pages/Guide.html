Chocolate gingerbread cake

Guide:

1. Mix the butter, cacao,suger, water and gingerbread Spice

2. Heat and stir the mix.

3. Let it cool for a bit.

4. Add Flour, vanilla suger, backingsoda and the eggs in a seperate Bowl.

5. Add both masses together and mix them.

6. pre heat the oven to 175 degree celsius and wait a bit.

7. Put the mass evenly on a bakingsheet and put it in the Oven

8. Keep it in the oven for about 30min
